Welcome to a new blog! This was completely unexpected.
Today, I spent a lot of my day promoting my brand new and first article post, The Rice of Babel: The Hardships of Linux Theming.
Well, something crazy happened. I got a notification from Touhou Wiki's Discord server, weird. I checked the ping and I saw a post talking about one of the worst case scenarios becoming a reality.
First, do you remember that Google used to have an informal motto? Don't be evil. Well, what Google apparently just did to Touhou Wiki IS EVIL.
I will quote what the Touhou Wiki admins wrote:

Hey @everyone; I wanted to inform you all about the situation re: Google searches.
A couple weeks ago the site started getting hammered heavily by the Google web crawler bot. Enabling Cloudflare verification for the US mitigated the issue, but then the site started dropping off Google search results.
Currently we have the site and Cloudflare configured to deal with bots in two main ways. For legitimate web crawlers (bots trawling across the Internet for search engines), the site generates index files to help assist those bots in figuring out the site's overall structure, to help them see everything on the site without getting stuck in dead-ends. For bots trying to scrape the site for AI purposes, we're using Cloudflare's AI mitigation and labyrinth functions, so those should not be an issue.
The problem this time is the possibility that the Google web crawler is now being used to scrape websites' content for AI purposes, even while being advertized to not do so. This is a really new problem and not something I or the wiki can resolve, unless someone like Cloudflare challenges Google over it.
THE SITE IS NOT DOWN; Google is essentially DDoSing the site with AI content scraping under the guise of legit web crawler activity. And of course the Google search results are suffering from it.
Knowing to access the site versus Fandom will be old hat for anyone in the community long enough, but those new to it likely will be relying on Google searches for access to information. If you see anyone new using searches or direcly using Fandom, let them know what to use instead.
I also recommend using the IndieWikiBuddy extension to redirect Fandom wiki links to Touhou Wiki itself.
Thanks for the understanding!
